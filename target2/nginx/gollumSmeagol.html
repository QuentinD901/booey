Be aware of Gollum and Sméagol

Gollum, a wretched creature once known as Sméagol, has long been consumed by the power of the One Ring. He will do anything to get it back, even if it means leading you into danger. Sméagol, on the other hand, is a pitiful figure, who reminds us of what Gollum once was. He may be more sympathetic, but even he is not to be trusted completely. You must be careful in this cave, for the ring's power is strong here. Will you explore and risk encountering these two creatures, or will you turn back and seek another path?

These two have created several hidden paths and caverns within this cave system. Use them to escape with the One Ring!
